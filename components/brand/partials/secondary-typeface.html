Secondary Typeface
Gold Coast Script

Gold Coast Script is Destination Gold Coast’s secondary typeface.

It is only used within advertising and some promotional materials as a visual differentiator to display tag lines and marketing messages.

Please note: External suppliers are not permitted to use this typeface on any branded promotions or materials. It is for internal use only.
